Barboza

Founded in 2012, Barboza is the sister showroom to Seattle’s legendary Neumos. Located in what was formerly an unfurnished basement and private event space, Barboza is a 200 capacity music venue featuring the hottest up-and-coming local and national acts. From local powerhouse acts to emerging buzz bands, Barboza is a hotbed of new talent. During the weekends we also host some of Seattle’s favorite dance nights, featuring a host of incredibly talented house and guest DJs.
